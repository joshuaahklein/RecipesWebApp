Chili and Garlic Chex Mix
Cook Time:
15 minutes
Servings:
18
Rating:
3
Ingredients
4 cups corn Chex or rice Chex
5 cups popped fat-free microwave popcorn
1/2 cup dry-roasted peanuts
2 Tbsp. canola or vegetable oil
3 Tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese
1 tsp. chili powder
1 tsp. garlic powder
Directions
In a large microwavable bowl mix cereal, popcorn, and peanuts.  Drizzle with oil and toss evenly until coated.
In a small bowl mix remaining ingredients.  Sprinkle over cereal mixture.  Toss until evenly coated.
Microwave uncovered on high 3 to 4 minutes stirring after each minute until crisp.  Spread on waxed paper to cool.  Store in airtight container.
