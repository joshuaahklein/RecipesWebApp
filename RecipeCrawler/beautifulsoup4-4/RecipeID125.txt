Mexican 7-Layer Dip
Rating:
2
Ingredients
2 cans refried beans
3 bunches green onions, chopped
1 (16 oz) carton avocado dip
1 can sliced black olives
1 (16 oz) sour cream, mixed with 1 PKG taco seasoning mix
2-3 chopped tomatoes
1-2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
Directions
Start with a layer of refried beans, layer ingredients in the order given, in pan or dish with sides, chill and serve cold.
