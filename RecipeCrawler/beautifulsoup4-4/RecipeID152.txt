Spiced Hot Tea
Rating:
5
Ingredients
1 (46-oz) can pineapple juice
6 cups water
1 quart water
15 whole cloves
1 (3-oz) pkg. Cherry gelatin
1 (8-oz) bottle lemon juice
6 tea bags
3 cinnamon sticks
1 Â½ cup sugar
1 (12-oz) can frozen orange juice (mix as directed)
Directions
Boil 6 cups water and pour over tea bags, steep until needed. Bring quart water to a boil, add cinnamon and cloves and simmer for 20 minutes. Add the remaining ingredients and bring to a boil. This can be served hot or cold. Store in the refrigerator.
