Cranberry Punch
Rating:
3
Ingredients
1 - 6 oz. can frozen lemonade
1 - 6 oz. can frozen orange juice
1 - 6 oz. can frozen pineapple juice
6 cups cranberry juice cocktail
5 cups cold water
1 - 28 oz. bottle ginger ale, 7-Up, or Sprite
Directions
Thaw juices. Combine with cranberry juice and water. Pour over ice in punch bowl. Pour ginger ale in just before serving. Float ice cream, sherbet, or fruit slices either plain or in an ice ring or individual ice cubes for a fancy look.
