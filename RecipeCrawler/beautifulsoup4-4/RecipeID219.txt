Hot Sausage Stuffing
Cook Time:
60 minutes
Servings:
10-12
Rating:
0
Ingredients
2 packages Hot Flavor Jimmy Dean Pork Sausage
8 oz. fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 1/2 cups diced onions
3/4 cup diced or coarsely grated carrots
1 1/2 cups diced celery
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 package (10 oz.) frozen spinach, thawed and drained
2 tsp. dried rosemary
2 tsp. poultry seasoning
1 tsp. black pepper
1 package (15 oz.) herbed dry bread crumbs
2 cups chicken broth
1 egg, lightly beaten
Directions
Preheat oven to 325-degrees F.  In a large saucepan, cook sausage over medium-high heat, stirring frequently until thoroughly cooked and no longer pink.
Add mushrooms, onion, carrots, celery, and garlic; cook 10 minutes or until onion becomes translucent, stirring frequently.
Transfer mixture to a large bowl; add remaining ingredients; stir well.  Pour into a buttered 4-quart casserole disk.  Bake covered for 60 minutes.
