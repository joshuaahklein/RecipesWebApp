Cheesy Taco Dip
Servings:
8 - 10
Rating:
1
Ingredients
1 pound ground beef
3/4 cup water
1 (1.25 ounce) envelope taco seasoning mix
1 (16 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
1 pound processed cheese spread
Directions
In a large skillet, brown ground beef. Drain fat. Stir in seasoning packet and water. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to simmer. Let cook for 5 minutes, stir occasionally. Place ground beef mixture into a slow cooker. Add tomatoes and cheese to the slow cooker. Cook on low for 1 hour.
