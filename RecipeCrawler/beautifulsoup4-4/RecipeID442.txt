White Chocolate Raspberry Cheesecake Bars
Prep Time:
20 minutes
Cook Time:
4 hours 48 minutes
Servings:
9
Rating:
5
Ingredients
12 Oreo cookies, finely crushed
2 Tbsp. butter, melted
3 squares white chocolate, divided
2 packages (8 oz. each) cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs
1/4 cup red raspberry preserves
Directions
Heat oven to 350-degrees F.  Mix cookie crumbs and butter; press onto bottom of 8-inch square pan.  Melt 2 chocolate squares as directed on package.
Beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla with mixer until blended.  Add melted chocolate; mix well.  Add eggs, 1 at a time, mixing on low speed after each just until blended.  Pour over crust.
Bake 25 to 28 minutes or until center is almost set.  Cool 5 minutes; spread with preserves.  Melt remaining chocolate square; drizzle over cheesecake.  Cool cheesecake.  Refrigerate 4 hours.  Cut into 18 bars.
