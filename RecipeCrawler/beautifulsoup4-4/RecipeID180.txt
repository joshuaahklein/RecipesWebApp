Chicken Paprika Skillet
Prep Time:
5 minutes
Cook Time:
20 minutes
Servings:
4
Rating:
1
Ingredients
1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
1 small onion, chopped
1 tsp. paprika
1 package (10.3 oz) Shells and Cheese with Bacon
1/4 cup sour cream
Directions
Heat 1 Tbsp oil in a large skillet on medium-high heat.  Add chicken and onion, cook and stir 5 minutes.  Add paprika; cook and stir an additional minute.
Add 2 cups water and Shell Pasta.  Bring to a boil; cover.  Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer 8 minutes.  Stir in contents of the bacon pouch; cover and continue cooling 5 minutes or until Shells are tender and most of the water is absorbed.
Stir in cheese sauce and sour cream until well blended.  Cook until heated through, stirring occasionally.
