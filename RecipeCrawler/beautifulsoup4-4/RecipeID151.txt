Russian Tea Mix
Rating:
0
Ingredients
¼ tsp. Cinnamon
1/8 tsp. Cloves (opt)
½ cup Tang
½ cup instant tea
½ pkg. Lemonade (unsweetened)
¼ cup sugar
Directions
Combine cinnamon, cloves, Tang, tea, lemonade and sugar in an airtight container. Put 1-2 spoonful's in a mug of hot water and stir.
