Roasted Garlic Teriyaki Burgers
Servings:
6
Rating:
4
Ingredients
2 pounds lean ground beef
1/4 cup Kikkoman Roasted Garlic Teriyaki Marinade & Sauce
Directions
Thoroughly combine ground beef and roasted garlic teriyaki sauce; shape into 6 or 8 patties.
Grill or broil 5 to 6 minutes on each side, or until thoroughly cooked, brushing with additional roasted garlic teriyaki sauce after turning.
