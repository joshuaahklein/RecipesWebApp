Crispy Garlic Rib Appetizers
Servings:
4
Rating:
0
Ingredients
2 pounds pork loin back ribs, sawed in half across bones
1/2 cup Kikkoman Teriyaki Marinade & Sauce
2 tablespoons dry sherry
2 cloves garlic, pressed
Directions
Cut ribs into 1-rib pieces.
Place in single layer in 9x13-inch microwave-safe baking dish; cover. Microwave on High 10 minutes; turn ribs over and rotate dish 1/2 turn. Microwave on High 5 minutes longer. Let stand 5 minutes.
Remove ribs from baking dish and place in large bowl.
Combine teriyaki sauce, sherry and garlic. Pour over ribs; toss to coat all pieces. Marinate 1 hour, turning ribs over frequently.
Place ribs, meaty side up, on rack of broiler pan.
Bake in 425Â°F. oven 15 minutes, or until brown and crispy.
