Super Easy Turkey Cutlets
Servings:
4
Rating:
3
Ingredients
2/3 cup Kikkoman Gourmet Teriyaki Quick & Easy Marinade
1 pound turkey breast slices
Directions
Pour quick & easy marinade over turkey in large plastic food storage bag; marinate 20 minutes, turning over once.
Broil turkey 2 minutes. Turn over; brush with additional quick & easy marinade. Cook 2 minutes longer, or until done.
