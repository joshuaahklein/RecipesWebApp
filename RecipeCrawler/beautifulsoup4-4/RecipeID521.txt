Southwest Salsa Burritos
Servings:
4 servings, 8 burritos
Rating:
4
Ingredients
1 lb. ground beef, pork, chicken, or turkey
1 tsp. chili powder
1 cup mild salsa
1 can (15 oz.) black beans, drained
8 soft wheat or flour tortillas (8-10 inches in diameter)
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
Condiments:
Chopped tomatoes
Shredded lettuce
Sliced avocados
Sour cream
Directions
In a large skillet cook meat, breaking with a fork, until all pink is gone.  Pour off drippings.  Stir in chili powder, salsa, beans, and salt to taste.
Microwave tortillas according to package directions.  Spoon meat mixture into center of tortillas.
Top with cheese and desired condiments.  Roll into burrito form.
