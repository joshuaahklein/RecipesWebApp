Parmesan Noodle Skillet
Cook Time:
15 minutes
Servings:
4
Rating:
1
Ingredients
2 cups dried medium noodles
12 oz. lean ground beef
1 cup purchased light Alfredo sauce
1 can (14 1/2 oz.) whole green beans, drained
1/4 cup chopped red sweet pepper
Directions
Cook noodles according to package directions; drain.
Cook beef in large skillet until brown; drain.  Stir in noodles, sauce, and 2 Tbsp. water.  Heat through.
Stir in beans and sweet pepper; heat through.  Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.
