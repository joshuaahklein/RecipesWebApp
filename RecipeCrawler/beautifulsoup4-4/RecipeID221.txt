Chocolate Peanut Butter Pie
Prep Time:
15 minutes
Servings:
8
Rating:
5
Ingredients
Pre-made Graham cracker crust
1 box instant chocolate pudding
2/3 cup Powdered Sugar
1/3 cup Peanut Butter
8 oz Cool Whip
Directions
Prepare pudding according to directions on box.  Mix powdered sugar and peanut butter.  Cover bottom of sugar RB mix in bottom of crust reserving some of the crumbly mixture to top cool whip with.  Add prepared pudding.  Top with Cool Whip then sprinkle with remaining PB sugar mixture.
