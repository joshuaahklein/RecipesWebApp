Taco Soup
Prep Time:
5 minutes
Cook Time:
25 minutes
Servings:
12, 1 cup servings
Rating:
5
Ingredients
1 lb. extra lean ground beef
1 onion, chopped
3 cans (15 1/2 oz. each) mild chili beans, undrained
1 can (14 1/2 oz.) whole tomatoes, undrained
1 can (14 1/2 oz.) corn, undrained
1 can (8 oz.) tomato sauce
1 package (1 1/4 oz.) taco seasoning mix
1 1/2 cups water
1 1/2 cups 2% shredded cheddar cheese
Directions
Brown beef with onions in a large saucepan; drain.
Add all remaining ingredients except cheese; stir, breaking up tomatoes.  Bring to boil.  Simmer on medium-low heat 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Serve topped with cheese.
