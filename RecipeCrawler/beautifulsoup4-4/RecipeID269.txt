The real reuben
Prep Time:
10 minutes
Cook Time:
3-4 minutes
Servings:
One
Rating:
3
Ingredients
2 slices dark rye bread
1/4 pound thinly sliced corned beef
3 ounces sauerkraut, drained
2 slices swiss cheese
1/4 cup thousand island
Directions
Place bread on baking sheet or broiling pan.  Layer corned beef sauerkraut and cheese on bread slices.  Broil on high heat 3-4 minutes until cheese has melted.  Serve hot with thousand island dressing.
