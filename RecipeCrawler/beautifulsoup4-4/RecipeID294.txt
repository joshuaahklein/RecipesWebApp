Ultimate Grilled Steak
Prep Time:
15 to 20 minutes
Cook Time:
8-10 minutes
Servings:
Two
Rating:
3
Ingredients
1/2 cup steak sauce
1/2 cup Balsamic Vinaigretta Dressing
2 small cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon dried oregano leaves
1 (8 ounce) beef rib eye steaks
Directions
Mix steak sauce, dressing, garlic and oregano.  Reserve 1/4 cup of the mixture.  Pour remaining 3/4 cup mixture over steaks in a resealable plastic bag, seal bag.  Refrigerate 30 minutes to marinate.  Preheat grill to med-high heat.  Drain steak; discard marinade.  Grill steak 4 to 5 minutes on each side or until medium doneness (160 degrees F.).  Serve drizzled with reserved 1/4 cup dressing mixture.
