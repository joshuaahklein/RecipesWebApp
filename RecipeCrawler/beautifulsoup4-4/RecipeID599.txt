Tri-color Rotini with Peppers and Gorgonzola Cheese
Prep Time:
25 minutes
Servings:
4
Rating:
0
Ingredients
1 box tri-color rotini
1 Tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
1 small red onion, cut julienne style
1 medium red bell pepper, diced
1 medium yellow bell pepper, diced
1 medium green bell pepper, diced
Salt and black pepper, to taste
1/2 cup Gorgonzola cheese, crumbled
1 tsp. parsley, finely chopped
Directions
Bring large pot of water to a boil.
In a large skillet, heat olive oil and saute onions for 2 minutes.
Add peppers and saute an additional 5 minutes.  Season with salt and pepper.
Cook pasta according to package directions.
Drain pasta, reserving 1/2 cup of the water.
Toss pasta with peppers and onions, and pasta water.
Fold in cheese and top with parsley.
