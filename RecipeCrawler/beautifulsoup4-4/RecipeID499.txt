Chili
Prep Time:
20 minutes
Cook Time:
8-9 hours
Servings:
6-8
Rating:
5
Ingredients
2 large onions, chopped
5-6 cloves garlic, minced
2 green peppers, chopped
2-3 pounds ground beef or buffalo, cooked and drained
2 14-ounce cans of red, black, or white beans, rinsed and drained
1 14-ounce can diced tomatoes
2 jalapenos, seeded and rinsed
1 Tbsp. cumin
1 tsp. cayenne pepper
1/2 cup beef broth
Directions
Add all ingredients to slow cooker.
Cover and cook on low for 8-9 hours or on high for 4-5 hours.
