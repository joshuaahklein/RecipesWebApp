Classic Mandarin Orange Salad
Prep Time:
10 minutes
Servings:
4
Rating:
4
Ingredients
1 package (7 oz.) baby spinach or other salad blend
1 can (11 or 15 oz.) mandarin oranges, drained
1/2 cup sliced ripe olives
1/2 cup sliced red onion
1/2 cup balsamic vinaigrette
1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
Directions
Combine spinach or salad blend, mandarin oranges, olives and onion in a large serving bowl.
Pour vinaigrette over salad; toss to evenly coat.  Top with feta cheese.
