Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken
Prep Time:
20 minutes
Cook Time:
8-10 hours
Servings:
4-6
Rating:
0
Ingredients
4-6 pound roasting chicken
1/2 cup onion, chopped
1-2 Tbsp butter
Juice of one lemon
1/2 tsp. Kosher salt
2 Tbsp. fresh parsley
1/2 tsp. dried thyme
1/3 tsp. paprika
Directions
Place the onion in the cavity of the chicken and rub the skin with butter.  Place chicken in slow cooker.
Squeeze lemon juice over chicken and sprinkle with remaining seasonings.  Cover and cook on low for 8-10 hours or on high for 4-5 hours.
