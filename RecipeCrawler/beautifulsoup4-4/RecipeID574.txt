Taco Fun Cups
Rating:
4
Ingredients
1 lb ground beef
1 packet taco seasoning mix
1 canister refrigerated buttermilk biscuits
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
shredded lettuce & diced tomatoes for garnish
Directions
Heat oven to 400F.  Cook meat with seasoning mix as directed on package.  Open biscuit canister and stretch each biscuit a little. Press each biscuit into the bottom and up the sides of 10 muffin cups. Fill with meat mixture and bake 12 minutes. Sprinkle with cheese and bake 2-3 minutes or until melted. Serve with shredded lettuce and diced tomato on top for garnish.
