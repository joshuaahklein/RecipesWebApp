Sweet and Sour Meatballs
Servings:
12
Rating:
5
Ingredients
2 pounds ground round (very lean)
2 cups dry breadcrumbs
2 eggs
1/2 cup minced onion
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
2 teaspoons salt
1 cup barbecue sauce
2 (12 ounce) jars pineapple preserves
Directions
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F .In a medium-size mixing bowl, mix barbecue sauce and pineapple preserves together. In a medium-size mixing bowl, combine meat, breadcrumbs, eggs, onions, salt, and parsley. Mix well and form into bite-size balls. Arrange the balls in a single layer in a 9x13 inch-baking dish. Pour the barbecue sauce mixture evenly over the meatballs. Bake for 30 to 45 minutes, until the meat is cooked.
