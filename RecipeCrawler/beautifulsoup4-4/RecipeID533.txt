Mango Tart
Cook Time:
7 minutes
Rating:
4
Ingredients
Fresh Mango, sliced
One banana
1 cup coconut cream
dash of vanilla
Vegan graham cracker crust
Directions
Press a vegan graham cracker crust into a tart pan.  Bake 7 minutes at 375-degrees.  Cool.
Blend one banana with 1 cup coconut cream and a dash of vanilla.  Pour into crust and refrigerate until cold.  Place mango slices on top.
