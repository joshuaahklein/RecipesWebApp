Ultimate 7-Layer Party Dip
Rating:
3
Ingredients
1 16oz can refried beans
1 tablespoon taco seasoning mix
1 cup sour cream
1 cup salsa
1 cup shredded lettuce
1 cup Mexican style shredded cheese
1/2 cup sliced green onions
2 tablespoons sliced pitted ripe olives (optional) OR 1/3 cup diced tomatoes (optional)
Tortilla chips for serving
Directions
Mix beans and taco seasoning mix. Spread into bottom of 9&quot; pie plate. Layer all remaining ingredients in order (except chips) over bean mixture. Cover and refrigerate until chilled.
