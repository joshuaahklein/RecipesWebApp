Soy-Jalapen~o Dip
Servings:
2 cups
Rating:
4
Ingredients
1 carton (8 oz.) dairy sour cream
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup finely chopped green onions
3 tablespoons Kikkoman Soy Sauce
1 to 2 tablespoons finely chopped jalape√±o peppers
Directions
Combine all ingredients until well blended.
Cover and refrigerate about 1 hour to blend flavors.
Serve with crackers, chips and cut-up vegetables, such as carrots, cucumber, celery and bell pepper.
Variations:
Soy-Jalapeno with Shrimp:Stir in 1 cup cooked bay shrimp (about 4 ounces), rinsed, well drained and chopped. Cover and refrigerate about 1 hour to blend flavors.
Soy-Jalapeno with Roasted Peppers:Stir in 1/2 cup roasted red bell peppers, rinsed, drained and chopped. Cover and refrigerate about 1 hour to blend flavors.
