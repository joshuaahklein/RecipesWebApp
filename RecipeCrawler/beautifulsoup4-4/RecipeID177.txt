SWEET  POTATO CASSEROLE
Prep Time:
45-60 minutes (mainly potato cooking)
Cook Time:
30-40 minutes
Servings:
10-12
Rating:
4
Ingredients
3 Cups Mashed Sweet Potatoes (4 fairly large cooked & peeled)
1 cup white sugar
½ teaspoon salt
3 eggs
3 Tablespoons margarine
½ cup milk
1-Tablespoon Vanilla
TOPPING
½ cup Dark brown sugar (packed)
1/3 cup Flour
1 Cup chopped pecans
3 Tablespoons Margarine
Directions
Mix potatoes, sugar, salt, eggs, margarine, milk and vanilla.  Pour in a greased 9 x 13 casserole. Bake at 400 degrees for 20 to 30 minutes or until firm.
Topping:
Mix until crumbly, sprinkle over potatoes. Bake 10 minutes longer at 400 degrees.
