Pot Roast
Prep Time:
20 minutes
Cook Time:
10-12 hours
Servings:
6-10
Rating:
0
Ingredients
2-4 pounds beef chuck pot roast
1/2 cup flour
Kosher salt and pepper
3 carrots, sliced
3 potatoes, quartered
3 onions, sliced
2 stalks celery, sliced
1 cup mushrooms, sliced
1 cup beef broth or wine
Directions
Coat roast with 1/2 cup flour, salt, and pepper.  Sear roast in a skillet on stove top (optional).
Place all vegetables in slow cooker, except mushrooms.  Add roast and spread mushrooms on top.  Pour in liquid.
Cover and cook on low for 10-12 hours or on high for 6-8 hours, or until tender.
