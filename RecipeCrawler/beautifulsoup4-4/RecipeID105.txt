Spaghetti with Cherry Tomatoes and Basil
Rating:
5
Ingredients
1 box (14.5 oz.)  Spaghetti
2 Tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
1 clove garlic, chopped
3 pints (6 cups) cherry tomatoes, halved
Salt and pepper, to taste
1 Tbsp. fresh basil, chopped or small whole leaves
1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
Directions
Cook pasta according to package directions.  Meanwhile, heat olive oil and garlic in a large skillet until garlic turns golden.
Add cherry tomatoes, salt and pepper.  Cook for 2 minutes.
Drain pasta and add to sauce mixture.  Add basil and toss over medium heat for 1 minute.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and serve.
