Triple Layer Mud Pie
Prep Time:
15 minutes
Servings:
10
Rating:
0
Ingredients
3 squares semi-sweet chocolate, melted
1/4 cup canned sweetened condensed milk
1 Oreo pie crust (6 oz.)
1/2 cup chopped pecans, toasted
2 cups cold 2% milk
2 packages (3.9 oz. each) Jello chocolate instant pudding
1 tub (8 oz.) Cool Whip topping, thawed, divided
Directions
Mix chocolate and condensed milk.  Pour into crust; sprinkle with nuts.
Beat 2% milk and pudding mixes with whisk 2 minutes; spoon 1 1/2 cups over nuts.  Stir half the Cool Whip into remaining pudding; spread over pudding in crust.  Top with remaining nuts.
Refrigerate 3 hours.
