Beef Barley Soup with Spinach
Cook Time:
2 - 2 1/4 hours
Servings:
5-8 servings
Rating:
5
Ingredients
1 1/2 lb. beef for stew, cut into 1-inch pieces
1 package (0.9 to 1.43 oz) dry onion-mushroom soup mix
1 tsp dried thyme
1/2 cup medium pearled barley
4 cups fresh baby spinach
Directions
Combine beef, soup mix, thyme, 5 cups water, 1/2 cup tsp salt and 1/4 tsp pepper in stockpot; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; cover tightly and simmer 1 hour.
Stir in barley; simmer, covered, 45 minutes to 1 hour or until barley is tender and beef is fork-tender.  Stir in spinach; remove from heat.  Let stand, covered, 5 minutes.
