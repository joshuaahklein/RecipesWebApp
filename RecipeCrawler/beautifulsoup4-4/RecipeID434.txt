Creamy Crab and Red Pepper Spread
Cook Time:
15 minutes
Servings:
18, or 2 1/4 cups
Rating:
0
Ingredients
2 green onions, thinly sliced, divided
1 tub (8 oz.) cream cheese
1 can (6 oz.) lump crab meat, drained
1/2 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1/2 cup finely chopped red peppers
1 Tbsp. Grey Poupon Dijon Mustard
Crackers
Directions
Reserve 2 Tbsp. onions.  Mix remaining onions with all remaining ingredients except crackers.  Cover.
Refrigerate at least 1 hour.
Sprinkle with reserved onions.  Serve with crackers
