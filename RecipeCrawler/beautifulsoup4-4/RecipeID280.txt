Grilled Cedar=Planked Salmon
Prep Time:
20 minutes
Cook Time:
20 minutes
Servings:
4 servings
Rating:
0
Ingredients
1 untreated cedar plank (14x7x1 inch)
1/2 cup Sun-Dried Tomato Vinaigrette Dressing
1/4 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
1/4 cup finely chopped sub-dried tomatoes
1 Tablespoon oil
1 (2 pound) salmon fillet, i inch thick
Directions
Immerse the plank in water, placing a weight on top of the plank to keep it submerged.  Soak at least 4 hours or overnight.
Preheat the grill to medium heat.
Mix dressing, parsley and tomatoes: set aside.
Brush top of cedar plank with oil: top with salmon.
Place on grill; cover grill with lid.
Grill 10 minutes.  Brush with dressing: continue grilling 10 minutes or until salmon flakes easly with a fork.
