Chex Honey Nut Cherry Crunch
Prep Time:
15 minutes
Servings:
28
Rating:
4
Ingredients
4 1/2 cups honey nut Chex
4 1/2 cups rice Chex
2 cups whole cashews
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1/4 cup honey
1 tsp. salt
1 cup dried cherries
1/4 cup toasted sesame seeds
Directions
In a large microwavable bowl mix cereal and cashews.
In a 2-cup microwavable measuring cup microwave brown sugar, butter, honey, and salt uncovered on high about 1 1/2 minutes, stirring after 1 minute until mixture comes to a boil.  Stir butter mixture.  Pour over cereal mixture, stir until evenly coated.
Microwave uncovered on high 4 minutes, stirring every minute.  Stir in cherries and sesame seeds.  Microwave an additional 3 minutes, stirring every minute.  Spread on waxed paper or foil to cool. Store in airtight container.
