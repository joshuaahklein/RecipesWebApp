Easy Chicken Pot Pie
Prep Time:
5 minutes
Cook Time:
30 minutes
Servings:
4
Rating:
3
Ingredients
1 can (10 3/4 oz.) cream of potato soup
3/4 cup milk
1/8 tsp. ground black pepper
1 cup frozen mixed vegetables, thawed
2 cans (4.5 oz.) premium chunk chicken breast, drained
1 egg
1 cup all-purpose baking mix
Directions
Preheat oven to 400-degrees F.
Mix soup, 1/4 cup milk, black pepper, vegetables, and chicken in a 9-inch pie plate.
Mix remaining milk, egg, and baking mix.  Pour over chicken mixture.  Bake 30 minutes or until hot and topping is golden.
