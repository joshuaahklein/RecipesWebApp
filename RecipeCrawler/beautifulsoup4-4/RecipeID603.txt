Cheesy Potato Casserole
Prep Time:
10 minutes
Cook Time:
45 minutes
Servings:
8
Rating:
3
Ingredients
1 can (10 1/2 oz) condensed cream of chicken soup
2 cups sour cream
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1/3 cup sliced green onion
1 package (30 oz.) frozen hash browns, thawed
2 cups crushed corn flakes cereal
1/4 cup butter, melted
Directions
Preheat oven to 350-degrees.  Coat 13x9-inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
In a large bowl, whisk together soup, sour cream, salt and pepper.  Stir in cheese, onion and hash browns until well mixed.  Spoon evenly on top of hash brown mixture.
In a medium bowl mix together cereal and butter.  Sprinkle evenly on top of hash brown mixture.
Bake uncovered for about 45 minutes or until hot and bubbly.  Allow to rest for 5 minutes before serving.  Garnish with additional sliced green onion if desired.
