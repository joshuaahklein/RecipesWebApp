Twice-Baked Potatoes
Prep Time:
20 minutes
Cook Time:
1 hour 40 minutes
Servings:
4
Rating:
0
Ingredients
4 baking potatoes
1/4 cup ranch dressing
1/4 cup sour cream
2 Tbsp. chopped fresh chives
1/4 lb. Velveeta cheese product, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
Directions
Heat oven to 400-degrees F.  Prick potatoes in several places with fork.  Bake 1 hour.  Reduce oven temperature to 350-degrees F.
Cut off thin slice from top of each potato.  Discard tops; scoop out centers, leaving 1/8th-inch-thick shells.  Mash potatoes.  Add dressing, sour cream, and chives; mash until fluffy.  Spoon into shells; top with Velveeta.  Place in shallow baking dish.
Bake 20 minutes or until heated through.
