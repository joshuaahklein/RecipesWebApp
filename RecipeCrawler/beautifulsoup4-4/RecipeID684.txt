Hot dog Chil
Rating:
4
Ingredients
1-pound Hamburger/ground Round/ground Sirloin
1-regular sized can Manwitch Sauce
Directions
Open package of hamburger (make sure thoroughly thawed if frozen), put in hot cast iron skillet (medium to medium high heat). Use wooden spoon to breakup hamburger. Make sure thoroughly cooked and brown. (Drain excess off Grease.)
Now open can Manwitch “Original Recipe” Sauce (regular sized can). Pour in skillet over hamburger, stirring thoroughly. Make sure hot, and cooked through.
Serve with your Hot Dogs.
