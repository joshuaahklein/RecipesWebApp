Polka Dot Pizza
Servings:
4
Rating:
4
Ingredients
1 (16 oz.) Italian bread shell
1/2 cup Kikkoman Sweet & Sour Sauce
3/4 cup thinly sliced onion
1-1/2 cups (6 oz.) shredded Monterey jack cheese
3 hot dogs, thinly sliced
Directions
Place bread shell on large pizza pan or cookie sheet. Spread sweet & sour sauce evenly over bread.
Arrange onion over sauce, then cheese; top with hot dog slices.
Bake in 450Â°F. oven 15 minutes, or until cheese melts. Serve immediately.
