Apple Cider Punch
Prep Time:
5 minutes
Rating:
3
Ingredients
6  cups apple cider
2  cups orange juice, cranberry-raspberry juice, or orange-mango juice
1/2  cup lemon juice
1  750-ml bottle sparkling white grape juice or sparkling wine
Directions
1. In punch bowl, large pitcher, or pitchers combine apple cider, orange juice, and lemon juice. Slowly add sparkling white grape juice or sparkling wine. Serve immediately. Makes 11-1/2 cups (about 15, 6-ounce servings).
2. Test Kitchen Tip: For an adult punch, use chilled champagne in place of the sparkling grape juice.
