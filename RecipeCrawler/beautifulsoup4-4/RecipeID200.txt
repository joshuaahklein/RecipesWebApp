Ambrosia
Prep Time:
15 minutes
Servings:
8
Rating:
1
Ingredients
5 large navel oranges, peeled, sectioned, and cut into 1-inch pieces
1 can (6 oz.) pineapple bits
1 C. flaked coconut
1/4 C. powdered
Directions
In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients.  Mix well.  Cover and refrigerate at least one hour.
