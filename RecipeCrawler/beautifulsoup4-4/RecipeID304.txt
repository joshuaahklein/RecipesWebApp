Old fashioned Apple Pie
Prep Time:
20-30 minutes
Cook Time:
50-60 minutes
Servings:
6-8
Rating:
0
Ingredients
2 (9 inch) unbaked pie crusts
7 cups peeled, cored and sliced apples
1 cup white sugar
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 tanlespoons butter
Directions
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.  In a bowl combine apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt.  Place mixture in a pastry-lined 9 inch pie plate.  Dot with butter and adjust top crust that has been vented.  Place in oven and bake at 425 degrees F for 10 minutes.  Turn oven temperature down to 275-300 degrees F and bake 40-50 minutes or until crust is golden brown and the apples are tender.  Let cool and serve.
