Orange-Pineapple Marshmallow Jello Salad
Rating:
2
Ingredients
1 box (6 oz) orange Jello-O
1 can (11 oz) Mandarin oranges
1 can (11 oz) pineapple tidbits
3 cups miniature marshmallows
Directions
Prepare orange Jell-O according to package directions.  When adding cold water add drained Mandarin oranges and pineapple tidbits.  Pour into casserole dish.  Cover with marshmallows.  Refrigerate to set before serving.
