Jalapeno Cornbread
Cook Time:
1 1/2-2 hours
Servings:
24
Rating:
2
Ingredients
2 boxes Jiffy Corn Bread Muffin Mix
2 C. grated cheddar cheese
1 15 oz. can cream style corn
2 C. sour cream
2/3 C. oil
1 medium onion chopped
4 eggs beaten
1/4 C. chopped jalapenos or more to taste
Directions
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Use 325 degrees if using a glass baking
dish.
Combine all ingredients in a large mixing bowl and mix well. Pour into a
13x9 pan and bake for 1 1/2 - 2 hours. Check after baking for about an hour. If necessary, cover edges with foil to prevent over browning along sides of dish. Use toothpick in center to test for doneness.
