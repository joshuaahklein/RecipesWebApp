Favorite Hot Cocoa
Rating:
5
Ingredients
1/2 cup sugar
1/3 cup hot water
1/4 cup Hershey's cocoa
4 cups milk
Dash of salt
3/4 t. vanilla extract
Directions
Mix sugar, cocoa, and salt in saucepan. Stir in water.
Cook and stir over medium heat until mixture boils. Boil and stir for 2 minutes.
Stir in milk and heat. DO NOT BOIL!
Remove from heat and add vanilla.
