Cheezy Chili Dip
Prep Time:
5 minutes
Cook Time:
6 minutes
Servings:
24, or 3 cups
Rating:
0
Ingredients
1 package (8 oz.) cream cheese, softened
1 can (15 oz.) chili
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
2 Tbsp. chopped cilantro
Directions
Spread cream cheese onto microwavable plate; top with chili and cheddar.
Microwave on HIGH 45 seconds to 1 minute or until cheddar is melted.  Sprinkle with cilantro.
