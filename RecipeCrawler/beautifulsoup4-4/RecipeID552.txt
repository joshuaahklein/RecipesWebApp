Meatballs Delight
Rating:
5
Ingredients
1/2 cup BBQ sauce
1 can (20 oz) pineapple chunks in juice, half drained
1 pkg (12.5 oz) frozen prepared meatballs
Serve over rice
Directions
Mix sauce ingredients (first two items) in a large skillet. Bring to a boil on medium heat, stirring frequently.  Once to a boil, stir in the meatballs and simmer on medium-low heat for 15 minutes or until the meatballs are heated through and sauce has thickened, stirring occasionally.  Serve over rice
