Hearty 7-Can Soup
Prep Time:
5-10 minutes
Cook Time:
15-20 minutes
Servings:
10-12
Rating:
1
Ingredients
1-1 1/2 lbs of ground hamburger or buffalo
2 cans of vegtable soup (beef or vegetarian)
2 cans of minestrone soup
2 cans of chili beans
1 can of rotel (tomato and green chili)
Directions
Brown hamburger and drain completely - add soup, beans and rotel with 3 soup cans of water and heat on medium until bowls.
Great with crackers, corn bread or flour tortillas
