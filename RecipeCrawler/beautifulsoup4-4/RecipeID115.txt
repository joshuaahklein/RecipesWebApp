Cheese Ball
Rating:
4
Ingredients
2 (8-oz.) packages cream cheese
1 TBSP Worcestershire sauce
1 TBSP. Accent seasoning
1 TBSP. Minced onion
1 pkg. Dried beef
Chopped pecans
Directions
Mix all ingredients, except the pecans. Refrigerate. After mixture sets up, shape into a ball and roll in the chopped pecans.
