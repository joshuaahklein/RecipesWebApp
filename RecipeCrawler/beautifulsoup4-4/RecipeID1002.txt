Chai Tea
Prep Time:
6 minutes
Cook Time:
4 minutes
Servings:
4
Rating:
4
No need to pay coffeehouse prices when itâ€™s this easy to make the classic Indian sweet and spicy tea.
Ingredients
2 cups water
4 bags black tea
2 cups milk
2 tablespoons honey
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
Directions
Heat water to boiling. Add tea bags; reduce heat. Simmer 2 minutes. Remove tea bags. Stir in remaining ingredients. Heat to boiling.
Stir with wire whisk to foam milk. Pour into cups.
