Grilled Salmon
Prep Time:
15 to 20 minutes
Cook Time:
15-20 monutes
Servings:
4 servings
Rating:
2
Ingredients
1 1/2 pound salmon fillets
lemon pepper to taste
garlic powder to taste
salt to taste
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/3 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup water
1/4 cup vegetable oil
Directions
Season salmon fillets with lemon pepper, garlic powder, and salt.  In a small bowel, stir together soy sauce. brown sugar, water, and vegetable oil until sugar is disolved.  Place fish in a large resealable plastic bag with the soy sauce mixture, seal, and turn to coat.  Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.  Preheat gril for medium heat.  Lightly oil grill grate.  Place salmon on the preheated grill, and discard marinade.  Cook salmon for 6 to 8 minutes per side, or until the fish flakes easly with a fork.
