Roasted Garlic Stir-Fry
Servings:
4
Rating:
2
Ingredients
3/4 pound boneless pork loin
1 tablespoon plus 1/4 cup Kikkoman Roasted Garlic
Teriyaki Marinade & Sauce, divided
1 tablespoon cornstarch
3 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
1 onion, sliced
2 ounces fresh snow peas, trimmed and julienned
3 cups shredded cabbage
1 red bell pepper, julienned
Hot cooked rice
Directions
Cut pork across grain into strips; coat with mixture of 1 Tbsp. roasted garlic teriyaki sauce and cornstarch. Let stand 15 minutes.
Heat 1-1/2 Tbsp. oil in hot wok or large skillet over high heat. Add pork and stir-fry 2 minutes; remove.
Heat remaining oil in same pan. Add onion and peas; stir-fry 2 minutes. Add cabbage and pepper; stir-fry 4 minutes. Add pork and remaining 1/4 cup roasted garlic teriyaki sauce; cook, stirring, to heat through.
Serve with rice.
