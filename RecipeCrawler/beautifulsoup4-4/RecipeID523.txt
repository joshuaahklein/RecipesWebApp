Easy Chicken and Rice
Prep Time:
5 minutes
Cook Time:
15 minutes
Servings:
2-4
Rating:
5
Ingredients
1 box (6.9 ounces) chicken flavored Rice-A-Roni
1 can (9.75 ounces) white chicken breast
2 1/2 cups water
2 Tbsp. butter or margarine
1/2 to 1 cup frozen vegetable of your choice (optional)
Black pepper
Directions
Prepare Rice-A-Roni according to package directions.  Once water and flavor packet has been added add the can of chicken breast.  Optionally you can also add 1/2 to 1 cup of frozen vegetables such as peas, corn, peas and carrots, green beans, mixed vegetables, or lima beans.  Bring to a boil, then simmer for 15-20 minutes or as the Rice-A-Roni directions state.
