Broccoli Salad
Rating:
0
Ingredients
1 Bunch Broccoli
½ cup red onion
Bacon Bits (real)
½ cup raisins
½ cup pecans
Dressing:
¾ cup Mayonnaise
¼ cup sugar
2 tbs. vinegar
Directions
Cut up broccoli, onion. Place in bowl.
Add (real) bacon bits, raisins, and pecans. Toss.
Mix mayonnaise, sugar, and Vinegar to make dressing. Pour over salad fixings. Mix. Let sit.
