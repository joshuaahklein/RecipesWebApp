Sausage Puffs
Rating:
4
Ingredients
3 cups Bisquick
1 lb. ground sausage (uncooked)
1 lb. melted cheddar cheese
Directions
Mix together in a large bowl, until well blended. Roll into 1-inch balls. Bake at 350 for 10-12 minutes. These can be frozen then thawed and reheated of frozen then baked.
