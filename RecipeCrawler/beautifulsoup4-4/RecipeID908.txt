Everyone's Favorite Pork Chops
Servings:
4
Rating:
0
Ingredients
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
6 pork chops, about 1/4 inch thick
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 medium tomatoes, chopped
1 medium onion, thinly sliced
3 tablespoons Kikkoman Soy Sauce
Hot cooked noodles (optional)
Directions
Combine first 3 ingredients; coat chops with mixture.
Brown chops on both sides in hot oil over medium-high heat. Sprinkle tomatoes and onion pieces over chops. Pour soy sauce and 1/3 cup water over all. Cover and simmer 45 minutes, or until tender, turning chops over once.
Serve over hot noodles, if desired.
