Taco Bake
Prep Time:
15 to 20 minutes
Cook Time:
20 to 25 minutes
Servings:
4 servings
Rating:
1
Ingredients
1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1 (16 ounce) can refried beans
2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
1 (16 ounce) jar salsa
Directions
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.  In a large heavy skillet over med-high heat, brown ground beef, and drain fat.  Mix in dry taco seasoning.  Spoon browned meat into a 9 x 13 inch glass baking dish.  Spoon a layer of refried beans over meat, then salsa.  Top with shredded cheese.  Bake about 20 to 25 minutes in the preheated oven.
