Baby Back Ribs
Prep Time:
10 minutes
Cook Time:
7-9 hours
Servings:
6-10
Rating:
5
Ingredients
2-4 racks of baby back ribs, cut into sections of 3 to 4
2-4 cups barbecue sauce
2-3 onions, chopped
Directions
Place ribs in slow cooker.  Top with onion and barbecue sauce.
Cover and cook on low for 7-9 hours or on high for 4-5 hours, or until meat is tender.
To serve, cut ribs between bones and coat with extra sauce as desired.
