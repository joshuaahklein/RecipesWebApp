Skillet Meatloaves
Cook Time:
15 minutes
Rating:
1
Ingredients
1/2 cup breadcrumbs
1/3 cup milk
1/3 cup chopped fresh parsley
1 large egg
3 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
1 small onion, finely chopped
1 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
3 cloves garlic, grated or finely minced
1 1/2 pounds meatloaf mix
salt & pepper
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/2 cup ketchup
1-2 tablespoons packed light brown sugar
1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
Directions
Mix the first 8 ingredients in a bowl; then add the meat and mix well.  Shape into six or seven 3-4&quot; oval loaves.  Salt & pepper your loaves.  Heat the vegetable oil in a skillet and once hot, add the loaves and brown about 3 minutes per side.  In a small bowl, whisk together the last 3 ingredients.  Brush a few tablespoonfuls over the meat.  Add 1/2 cup water to the skillet, cover and simmer over low heat until cooked through, about 15 minutes.
