Libby's Simply Pumpkin, Orange & Ginger Nectar
Prep Time:
3 minutes
Servings:
2
Rating:
1
Ingredients
1/2 cup LIBBY'S 100% Pure Pumpkin
2 cups orange juice
1/2 to 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger or more to taste
Directions
COMBINE pumpkin, orange juice and ginger in small pitcher; stir until smooth. If not drinking right away, cover and refrigerate any remaining. This nectar is best when consumed within 2 days.
TIPS:
• Recipe can easily be halved for a single serving to be mixed in your glass.
• If fresh ginger is not on hand, substitute 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon of ground ginger.
• Pumpkin may be stored, up to 1 week in the refrigerator, in a resealable plastic container.
