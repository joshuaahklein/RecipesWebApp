Swiss Steak
Rating:
3
Ingredients
2 round steaks, about 1/2-lb. each
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
2 carrots, sliced
1/2 tsp. ground black pepper
1 large onion, thinly sliced cut 3/4-inch thick
2 stalks celery, chopped
1 can (15-oz.) tomato sauce
Directions
Cut round steak into serving pieces.  Place flour, salt, and pepper in plastic bag.  Add steak pieces, a few at a time, shake to cover with flour mixture.  Place onion slices in bottom of crockpot; add meat on top.  Top with carrots and celery and cover with tomato sauce.  Cover and cook on low for 8 to 10 hours (3 to 5 hours on high.)
