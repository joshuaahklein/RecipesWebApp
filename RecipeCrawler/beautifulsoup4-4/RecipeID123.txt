Fruit Dip
Rating:
4
Ingredients
1 (8 ounce) jar marshmallow crème
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
2 tablespoons frozen orange juice concentrate
Directions
In a medium size-serving bowl, mix together marshmallow crème, cream cheese, and orange juice. Refrigerate and serve chilled. Good with grapes or strawberries.
