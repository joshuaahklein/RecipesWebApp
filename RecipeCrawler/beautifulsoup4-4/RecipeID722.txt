Easy Cheesy Sausage & Potato Bake
Prep Time:
10 minutes
Cook Time:
1 hour
Servings:
4
Rating:
0
Ingredients
1 pkg. (20 oz.) refrigerated diced potatoes
1 pkg. (16 oz.) frozen bell pepper and onion strips, thawed, drained
1 tub (10 oz.) Philadelphia Original Cooking Creme
1/2 tsp. smoked paprika
1 cup Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese, divided
1/2 lb. smoked turkey sausage, thinly sliced
Directions
Heat oven to 375Â°F.
Combine first 4 ingredients and 1/2 cup Cheddar in 2-qt. casserole sprayed with cooking spray; top with sausage. Cover.
Bake 30 min.; top with remaining Cheddar. Bake, uncovered, 20 min. or until heated through. Let stand 5 min. before serving.
