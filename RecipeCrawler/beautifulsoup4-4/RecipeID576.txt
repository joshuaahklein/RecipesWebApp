Parmesan Roasted Potatoes
Rating:
0
Ingredients
2 lbs red potatoes, unpeeled and cut into 1&quot; chunks
3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1 1/2 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1 cup grated parmesan
8 sprigs fresh thyme OR 3 teaspoons italian seasoning
Directions
Combine all ingredients in a bowl.  Transfer into a greased 9x13&quot; dish. Bake at 350 for an hour, stirring once, until golden brown and crisp.
