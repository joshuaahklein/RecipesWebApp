Chili Mac Skillet
Prep Time:
5 minutes
Cook Time:
12 minutes
Servings:
4
Rating:
2
Ingredients
12 oz. ground beef
1 can (15 1/4 oz.) whole kernel sweet gold and white corn, drained
1 can (14 1/2 oz.) diced tomatoes
1 can (15 oz.) red kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (4 oz.) diced green chilies, drained
3/4 cup dried elbow macaroni
2 tsp. chili powder
1 cup water
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese (optional)
Directions
Cook beef in large skillet until brown; drain.  Stir in corn, undrained tomatoes, beans, chilies, uncooked macaroni, chili powder, and 1 cup water.
Bring to a boil; reduce heat.  Simmer, covered, 12 to 15 minutes or until macaroni is tender.
Sprinkle with 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese, if desired.  Cover and let stand 5 minutes.
