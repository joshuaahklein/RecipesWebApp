Crab Spread
Servings:
12
Rating:
2
Ingredients
1 (8 ounce) package cream
cheese, softened
1 (8 ounce) package sour cream
1 (8 ounce) package
pre-cooked crab meat, flaked
1/4 teaspoon lemon or lime juice
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
Directions
In a large bowl, mix together cream cheese, sour cream, crab meat, lemon or lime juice, and garlic salt. Chill before serving.
