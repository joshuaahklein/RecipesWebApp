Herbed Turkey Breast
Rating:
3
Ingredients
1 turkey breast (4 to 5 lbs.) fresh or thawed
2 Tsp. butter or margarine
1/4 cup garden vegetable-flavored whipped cream cheese
1 Tsp. soy sauce
1 Tsp. fresh minced parsley
1/2 tsp. dry basil leaves
1/2 tsp. rubbed sage
1/2 tsp. dry thyme leaves
1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
Directions
Place turkey in crockpot.  Combine remaining ingredients and brush over turkey.  Cover and cook on low 10 to 12 hours (or high 5 to 6 hours.)
