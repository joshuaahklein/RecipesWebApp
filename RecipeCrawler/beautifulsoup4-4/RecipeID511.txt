Banana Bread
Servings:
2 loaves
Rating:
2
Ingredients
1 1/2 cups sugar
1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups very ripe bananas (3 to 4 medium)
1/2 cup buttermilk
1 tsp. vanilla
2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
1 cup chopped nuts (optional)
Directions
Preheat oven to 350-degrees.  Grease bottoms only of loaf pans.
In large bowl, mix sugar and butter.  Add eggs, bananas, buttermilk, and vanilla; beat with electric mixer on medium speed until smooth.  Stir in flour, baking soda, salt, and nuts just until moistened.  Pour batter evenly into pans.
Bake 8-inch loaves 1 hour or 9-inch loaves 1 hour 15 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.  Cool 10 minutes.  Loosen sides of loaves from pans; remove from pans to cooling rack.  Cool about 1 hour before slicing.
