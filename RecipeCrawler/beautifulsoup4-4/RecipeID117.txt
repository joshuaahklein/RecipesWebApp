Chicken Puffs
Rating:
1
Ingredients
8-oz crescent rolls
8- oz cream cheese
2 cups cooked chicken
2 tbs. Butter, soft
1/4 tsp. Salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
2 tbs. milk
1 egg white
Directions
Blend cheese & butter until smooth. Add chicken salt pepper & milk. Separate crescent dough into 4 rectangles. Press together at seams. Top each with 1/2 cup of chicken mixture. Fold over and pinch ends together to form pouch. Brush w/ egg white. Bake on a cookie sheet at 350 for 20-25 minutes.
