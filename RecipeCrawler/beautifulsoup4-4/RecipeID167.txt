Perfect Chocolate Frosting
Servings:
2 cups
Rating:
0
Ingredients
1 stick (1/2 cup) butter
2/3 cup Hershey's cocoa
3 cips powdered sugar
1/3 cup milk
1 t. vanilla extract
Directions
Melt butter. Stir in cocoa.
Alternatly add powdered sugar and milk, beating on medium speed to spreading consistency. Add more milk if needed.
Stir in vanilla.
