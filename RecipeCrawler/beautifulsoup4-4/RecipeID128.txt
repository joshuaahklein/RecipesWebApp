Picanti Dips
Rating:
1
Ingredients
1 jar dried chipped beef
1 pkg. flour tortilla's (large)
1 8oz cream cheese
1 small can chopped black olives
Directions
Finely chop the dried beef, and mix with the black olives and pre-softened cream cheese. Spread thinly over tortillas, and roll into a roll. Slice and dip in picante sauce, prior to eating.
