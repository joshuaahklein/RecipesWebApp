Parker Family Vegetable Beef Stew
Prep Time:
20 - 40 minutes
Cook Time:
8 - 12 hours
Servings:
a lot
Rating:
4
A hearty vegetable beef stew, cooked slow for perfection.
Ingredients
2 lbs beef stew meat
2 lbs frozen or fresh mixed vegetables (green beans, peas, carrots, corn)
2 large Idaho potatoes
8 cups (64 oz) beef broth
2 Tsp onion powder -or- 1/4 cup chopped onion
salt/pepper to taste
Directions
Chop potatoes into 1-inch or smaller pieces.  Place chopped potatoes on bottom of crockpot.  Put mixed vegetables on top of potatoes in crockpot.  Cut beef stew meat into 1-inch or smaller pieces; place on top of mixed vegetables and potatoes already in crockpot.  Sprinkle onion powder, salt, and pepper on top of meat.  Pour 8 cups of beef broth on top of items already placed in crockpot.  Cover and cook on low for 8 to 12 hours; or until done.  Stir mixture together after about 6 or 7 hours.  Stir more frequently closer to serving.
Makes about 5 1/2 quarts of stew.
