Szechwan Shrimp
Prep Time:
15 minutes
Cook Time:
15-20 minutes
Servings:
3-4
Rating:
2
Ingredients
4 tablespoons water
2 tablespoons ketchup
1 tablespoon soy sauce
2 teaspoons cornstarch
1 teaspoon honey
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/4 cup sliced green onions
4 cloves garlic, minced
12 ounces cooked shrimp
Directions
In a bowl, stir together water, ketchup, soy sauce, cornstarch, honey, crushed red pepper and ground ginger.  Set aside.  Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.  Stir in green onions and garlic: cook 30 seconds.  Stir in shrimp, and toss to coat with oil.  Stir in sauce.  Cook and stir until sauce is bubbly and thickened.
