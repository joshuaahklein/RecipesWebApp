Caprese Chicken with Bacon
Rating:
5
Ingredients
8 bacon strips
2 tomatoes, sliced
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 tablespoon olive oil
6 fresh basil leaves, thinly sliced
1/3 teaspoon salt & pepper
4 slices mozzarella cheese
Directions
Place chicken in an ungreased 13x9‚Äù baking pan; brush with oil and sprinkle with salt & pepper. Top with tomatoes and basil; then wrap each in two bacon strips, arranging bacon in a crisscross. Bake uncovered at 400 degrees for 30-35 minutes, or until a meat thermometer reads 165.  Top with cheese and bake a couple of minutes longer until it is melted.
