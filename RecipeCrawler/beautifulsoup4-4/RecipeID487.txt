Directions

SAUCE:
Sautee onion and garlic until brown. Add beer and brown gravy and reduce by half. Add raisins. Cook for another 10 minutes. Mix in 2 oz. butter and salt and pepper. Add steamed spinach underneath chicken for a garnish. Pour sauce over chicken and top with lots of chopped chives.
