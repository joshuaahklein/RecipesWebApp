Apple-Walnut Salad
Prep Time:
15 minutes
Cook Time:
chill 1 hour
Servings:
6-8
Rating:
3
Ingredients
1  6-ounce package fresh baby spinach
1  medium Granny Smith apple, cored and cut into bite-size chunks
1  medium Braeburn apple, cored and cut into bite-size chunks
1  cup walnut halves, toasted
1/2  cup raisins or golden raisins
Tangy Honey Mustard Dressing (see recipe, below)
Directions
1. Combine spinach, apples, walnuts, and raisins in a large salad bowl. Pour dressing over spinach mixture; gently toss to coat. Serve immediately.
2. Makes: 4 servings
3. Tangy Honey Mustard Dressing: Combine 1/2 cup bottled honey dijon salad dressing, 1 teaspoon cider vinegar, and 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving. Makes 1/2 cup.
