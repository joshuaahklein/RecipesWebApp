Sweet & Spicy Honey Nut Chex Mix
Prep Time:
15 minutes
Cook Time:
15 minutes
Servings:
12
Rating:
5
Ingredients
4 cups Honey Nut Chex cereal
1/2 cup dry-roasted peanuts
1/3 cup roasted salted sunflower nuts or hulled pumpkin seeds
2 Tbsp. honey
1 Tbsp. warm water
3/4 tsp. salt
3/4 tsp. ground cumin
1/2 tsp. ground red pepper (cayenne)
3/4 cup raisins
Directions
In large microwavable bowl, mix cereal, peanuts and sunflower nuts; set aside.
In small bowl, mix honey and warm water.  Stir in salt, cumin, and red pepper until blended.  Pour over cereal mixture, stirring until evenly coated.
Microwave uncovered on high for 3 minutes, stirring every minute.  Stir in raisins.  Microwave 1 minute longer.  Spread on wax paper or foil to cool.  Store in airtight container.
