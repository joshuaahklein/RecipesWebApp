Spicy Cheeseburger Dip
Prep Time:
5 minutes
Cook Time:
10 minutes
Servings:
36, or 4 1/2 cups
Rating:
3
Ingredients
1 lb. (16 oz.) Velveeta, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
1 can (10 oz.) Ro*tel diced tomatoes and green chilies, undrained
1 cup shredded Mozzarella cheese
1/2 lb. ground beef, cooked, drained
1/2 cup sliced green onion
Directions
Mix all ingredients except onions in a large microwavable bowl.
Microwave on HIGH 5 minutes or until Velveeta is completely melted, stirring after 3 minutes.  Stir in onions.
