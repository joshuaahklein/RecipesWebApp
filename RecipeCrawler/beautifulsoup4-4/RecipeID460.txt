Honey Glazed Baby Carrots
Prep Time:
5 minutes
Cook Time:
15 minutes
Rating:
5
These have been called candied carrots and are really easy to make.
Ingredients
2-3 cups small baby carrots
1/2 cup butter
1 Tbsp. brown sugar
1 Tbsp. honey
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
Directions
In a medium saucepan melt butter.  Add brown sugar, honey, cinnamon, and nutmeg.  Mix together.  Add carrots.
Cover and cook on medium low to medium heat for about 15 minutes; stirring occasionally.  When finished cooking the carrots should be firm - not mushy.  Remove from saucepan and serve.
