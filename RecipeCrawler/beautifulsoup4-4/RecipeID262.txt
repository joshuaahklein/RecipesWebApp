Fiesta Chopped Salad
Prep Time:
10-15 minutes
Servings:
4
Rating:
3
Ingredients
1 (12 ounce) bag salad greens
1 (6 ounce) package Southwestern Seasoned Chicken Breast Strips
1 cup Natural Three Cheese Crumbles
1 cup coarsely broken tortilla chips
1 avocado, peeled, pitted and chopped
1/4 cup chopped red onions
1/2 cup Ranch Dressing
Directions
1.  Toss greens with all remaining ingredients except dressing.  2.  Add dressing; mix lightly.  3.  Serve immediately
