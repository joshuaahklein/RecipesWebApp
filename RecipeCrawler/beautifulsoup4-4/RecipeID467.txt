Red Curry Shrimp
Prep Time:
20 minutes
Cook Time:
15 minutes
Servings:
2 to 4
Rating:
4
Ingredients
1/2 lb. fresh uncooked shrimp, shelled and deveined
1 can (14 fl. oz.) coconut milk
1/3 cup water
1/2 cup frozen peas or other vegetable
1/2-1 Tbsp. red curry paste
1-2 Tbsp. fish sauce
2 Tbsp. brown sugar
Directions
In a large saucepan simmer coconut milk and red curry paste over medium heat for 5 minutes.
Add fish sauce, brown sugar, water, and frozen peas.  Simmer 10 minutes stirring occasionally.
Stir in fresh shrimp and continue cooking 3 to 5 minutes or until shrimp are cooked.
Serve with jasmine rice.
