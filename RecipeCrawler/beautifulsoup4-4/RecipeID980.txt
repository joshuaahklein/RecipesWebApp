Sopes Verdes
Servings:
12
Rating:
1
Ingredients
1 Tbsp. vegetable oil
12 corn sopes
1 can (16 oz) traditional refried beans
8 oz. chorizo sausage
1 can (7 oz) salsa verde
1/2 cup chopped yellow onion
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
1/4 cup crumbled Cotija or feta cheese
Directions
Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat.  Fry sopes on each side until golden brown.  Press each in the middle to shape into a bowl, if necessary.
Heat beans in saucepan over medium heat until warm, stirring occasionally.  Place chorizo in skillet over medium heat and cook 5 minutes or until well done, stirring occasionally.  Drain if necessary.
Divide beans equally (about 2 Tbsp) between each sope and spread to cover.  Top each with about 1 Tbsp  chorizo.  Drizzle each with salsa verde and top with onopn, cilantro, and cheese.
