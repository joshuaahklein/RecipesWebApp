Baked Bean
Rating:
5
Ingredients
1 lb hamburger
1 Cup (approx) Chopped onions
1 Cup (approx) green peppers
32 ounces pork & Beans
1 Cup brown sugar
2 tsp mustard
1 Cup Ketchup
1 Dash of Texas Pete
1 Dash of Worchester Sauce
Directions
Brown the hamburger, with onions, and green peppers. Add in rest of ingredients with the beans and mix, then stir in the hamburger mixture and mix well.
Pour Bean mixture into 9 by 12 rectangular casserole dish.
Bake in 350ยบ Oven for minimum of 30 minutes.
