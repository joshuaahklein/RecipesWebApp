Sausage and Meatballs
Prep Time:
20 minutes
Cook Time:
7-8 hours
Servings:
8-10
Rating:
5
Ingredients
3-5 pounds sausage and meatballs, uncooked
6 cloves garlic, peeled and chopped
1 28-ounce can crushed tomatoes
1-2 28-ounce cans plum tomatoes in juice, chopped coarsely
1 bunch fresh basil
Cooked pasta
Grated cheese
Directions
Brown sausage and meatballs in a skillet on stove top (optional).
Add all ingredients to slow cooker except pasta and grated cheese.
Cover and cook on low for 7-8 hours or on high for 4-5 hours.
Serve with cooked pasta and grated cheese.
