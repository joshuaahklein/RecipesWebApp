Lea & Perrins Meatloaf
Prep Time:
10 minutes
Cook Time:
1 hour
Servings:
8
Rating:
4
Ingredients
2 pounds ground beef
2 eggs
1 cup dry plain bread crumbs
1/3 cup Lea & Perrins Worcestershire sauce
1/3 cup finely chopped onion
1/4 cup tomato ketchup
Directions
Preheat oven to 350-degrees.
In large bowl, combine all ingredients.  In a 13x9-inch baking or roasting pan, shape into a loaf.  Sprinkle top with additional Worcestershire sauce.
Bake, uncovered, 1 hour or until done.
Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
