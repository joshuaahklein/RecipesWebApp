Egg Drop Soup
Prep Time:
5 minutes
Cook Time:
15 minutes
Servings:
2-3
Rating:
1
A classic Chinese staple soup.  Very easy to make and tasty.
Ingredients
3 cans chicken/vegetable broth/stock
1 green onion, chopped fine
4 raw eggs, blended together
1 tsp. cornstarch or flour
Black pepper to taste
Salt to taste
Directions
Heat broth/stock with green onion and other seasonings to boiling.  Mix cornstarch/flour with water in a cup; add to broth.  Heat until thickened.
Remove from heat.  Add raw eggs, stirring with a fork.  Stir rapidly for small pieces or slowly for longer, thicker threads.
