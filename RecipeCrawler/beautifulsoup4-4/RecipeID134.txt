Spinach Dip
Rating:
0
Ingredients
1 cup sour cream
1 cup Miracle Whip (can use real mayonnaise if preferred)
1 pkg. Knorr Swiss vegetable soup mix
3 green onions, chopped fine
1 can (8 ounces) water chestnuts, drained, chopped
1 pkg. frozen chopped spinach, thawed, drained well and patted dry
Directions
Mix all together and chill thoroughly. Serve with Hawaiian bread or crackers or vegetables. Or serve in a hollowed out bread bowl.
