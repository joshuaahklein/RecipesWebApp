Italian Meatball Soup
Prep Time:
5 minutes
Cook Time:
10 minutes
Servings:
4
Rating:
0
Ingredients
8 oz. refrigerated or frozen Italian-seasoned or other meatballs
1 can (15 oz.) sweet peas, drained
1 can (14 1/2 oz.) diced tomatoes with basil, garlic, and oregano
1 can (14 1/2 oz.) beef broth
Fresh basil leaves (optional)
Directions
Combine all ingredients in large saucepan.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat.  Simmer, uncovered, about 10 minutes or until meatballs are heated through.  Garnish with fresh basil, if desired.
