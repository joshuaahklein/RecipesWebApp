Fiesta Corn Salad
Prep Time:
15-20 minutes
Cook Time:
none
Servings:
6-8
Rating:
3
Ingredients
2 – Cans Shoepeg Corn (must be shoepeg)
1-bundle of green onions (chopped)
¼ - Green Bell Pepper (chopped)
1 – Large Tomato (chopped)
Seven Seas Creamy Italian Dressing
Directions
Mix, cover with Italian dressing – chill and serve.
Best chilled over-night
