Mushroom Appetizer Squares
Cook Time:
20-25 minutes
Servings:
2 dozen
Rating:
3
Ingredients
1 8oz can refrigerated crescent rolls
2 cups mushroom slices
1/4 cup margarine, melted
1/2 cup Monterey jack cheese
1/2 teas oregano leaves
1/4 teas onion salt
Directions
Separate dough into 2 long rectangles. Press onto bottom and 1/2 inch high around sides of 13x9 inch pan. Toss mushrooms with margarine; arrange on dough. Sprinkle with cheese and seasonings. Bake at 375 degrees, 20-25 minutes. Cut into squares.
