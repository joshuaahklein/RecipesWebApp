Heart-Healthy Chicken Salad Sandwich
Prep Time:
10 minutes
Servings:
4 sandwiches
Rating:
2
Ingredients
2 Tsp. nonfat plain yogurt
2 Tsp. light mayonnaise
1/3 cup chopped celery
2 Tsp. finely chopped onion
1 can (9.75 oz.) white premium chunk chicken breast in water, drained
8 slices whole wheat bread
8 slices tomato
4 lettuce leaves
Directions
Stir yogurt, mayonnaise, celery, onion, and chicken in a bowl.
Divide chicken mixture between 4 bread slices.
Top with tomato, lettuce, and remaining bread slices.
