Green Bean abd Onion Saute
Prep Time:
5 minutes
Cook Time:
10 minutes
Servings:
4
Rating:
4
Ingredients
1 small onion, cut into thin wedges
2 tsp. olive oil
1 can (14 1/2 oz.) cut green beans, no salt added, drained
1 small tomato, cut into thin wedges
2 tsp. chopped fresh oregano or 1/4 tsp. dried oregano, crushed
Directions
Cook and stir onion in hot oil in medium saucepan over medium-high heat for 4 minutes or until tender.
Add beans, tomato, and oregano.  Heat through.  Transfer into a serving dish.
