Chicken Wings Teriyaki
Servings:
24
Rating:
5
Ingredients
1/2 cup teriyaki sauce
1/4 cup Holland House Sherry Cooking Wine
2 tablespoons oil
2 tablespoons honey
1 teaspoon finely chopped peeled ginger root
1/2 teaspoon five spice powder
1/4 teaspoon sesame oil
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 lb. Chicken wings, tips removed, cut in half
1/4 cup sliced green onions
1 tablespoon sesame seed toasted, if desired
Directions
Heat oven to 375-degrees F. In small bowl, combine all sauce ingredients; mix well. Pour into 13x9-inch pan. Add chicken wings, turning to coat all sides. Bake at 375Â°F for 35 to 45 minutes or until chicken is tender and no longer pink, turning once and basting occasionally with sauce. Sprinkle with green onions and sesame seed.
