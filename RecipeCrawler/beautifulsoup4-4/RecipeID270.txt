Watermelon Salsa
Prep Time:
15 minutes
Servings:
2-3
Rating:
3
Ingredients
2 cups seeded and coarsely chopped watermelon
2 tablespoons chopped onion
3 tablespoons seeded and chopped Anaheim Chili
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
Directions
In a serving bowl mix together the watermelon, onion and chili pepper. Season with the balsamic vinegar and garlic salt.  Cover and refridgerate for at least one hour to blend flavors.
