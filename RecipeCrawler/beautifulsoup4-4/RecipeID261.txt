Savory Vegetables
Prep Time:
10 minutes
Cook Time:
10 minutes
Servings:
4
Rating:
0
Ingredients
1 cup chicken broth
3 cups cut-up vegetables
Directions
Heat broth and vegetables in 2-quart saucepan over high heat to a boil.  Reduce heat to low.  Cover and cook for 5 minutes or until vegetables are tender-crisp.  Drain.
