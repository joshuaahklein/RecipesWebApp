Creamy Chicken Pasta with Broccoli Rabe
Prep Time:
10 minutes
Cook Time:
28 minutes
Servings:
4
Rating:
3
Ingredients
2 cups campanelle pasta, uncooked
4 cups cut-up broccoli rabe (3-inch pieces)
1 Tbsp. olive oil
1 lb. boneless skinless chicken breast, cut into 1-1/2 inch pieces
4 cloves garlic, chopped
1 tub (10 oz.) Philadelphia Italian Cheese and Herb Cooking Creme
1/2 tsp. fresh thyme( 6 sprigs)
Directions
Cook pasta in large saucepan as directed on package, adding broccoli rabe to the boiling water for the last 3 min.
Meanwhile, heat oil in large skillet on medium-high heat. Add chicken and garlic; cook 6 to 8 min. or until chicken is done.
Drain pasta mixture. Add to chicken mixture with cooking cream and thyme; stir. Cook 2 to 3 min. or until heated through, stirring frequently.
