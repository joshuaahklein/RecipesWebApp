Salsa Chicken
Prep Time:
15 to 20 minutes
Cook Time:
30 to 40 minutes
Servings:
4-6
Rating:
4
Ingredients
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts halves
4 teaspoons taco seasoning mix
1 cup salsa
1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
2 Tablespoons sour cream (optional)
Directions
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.  Place chicken breasts in a lightly greased 9 x 13 inch baking dish.  Sprinkle taco seasoning on both sides of chicken breasts, and pour salsa over all.  Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 25 to 35 minutes, or until chicken is tender and juicey and its juices run clear,  Sprinkle chicken evenly with cheese, and continue baking for an additional 3 to 5 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.  Top with sour cream if desired, and serve.
