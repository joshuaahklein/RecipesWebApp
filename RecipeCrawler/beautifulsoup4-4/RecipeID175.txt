CRANBERRY SALAD
Prep Time:
45-60 min but worth it
Cook Time:
none
Servings:
10-12
Rating:
0
Ingredients
2 cups fresh cranberries, Ground
2 cups apples, Ground
2 cups crushed pineapple, well drained-reserve syrup
2 cups white sugar
1 cup of pineapple syrup
2 cups Pecans, chopped
2 small boxes Black Cherry Jell-O
1-cup cold water
2 cups hot water
Directions
Grind cranberries and apples.  Drain pineapple and cover with sugar set to the side.  Mix hot water with Jell-O to dissolve completely, add cold water and pineapple juice.
Mix cranberries, apples, pineapples and pecans into Jell-O and refrigerate over night.  Optional â€“ garnish top with chopped pecans or miniature marshmallows. Can be chilled in a 9x13 Pyrex dish, cut into square and served on a lettuce leave or chilled in a glass-serving dish.
