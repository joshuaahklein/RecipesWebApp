Beer Biscuits
Cook Time:
15-20 monutes
Rating:
3
Ingredients
4 C. Bisquick mix
2 Tbsp. sugar
1 (12 oz.) can beer
Directions
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix all ingredients together, and either drop or roll out and place onto a greased cookie sheet. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes or until golden brown.
