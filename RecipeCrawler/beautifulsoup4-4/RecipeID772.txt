Sesame Cream Spread
Servings:
4-6
Rating:
1
Ingredients
1 package (3 oz.) cream cheese
2 tablespoons Kikkoman Soy Sauce
2 tablespoons sesame seed, toasted
Assorted crackers
Directions
Place cream cheese in shallow dish. Pour soy sauce over cream cheese; let stand at room temperature about 2 hours, turning over occasionally.
Remove cream cheese from soy sauce and &quot;roll&quot; in sesame seed.
Cover carefully with plastic wrap and chill in refrigerator until ready to serve.
Serve with crackers.
