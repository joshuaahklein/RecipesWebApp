Samuel Adams Boston Lager Thick Cut Pizza
Rating:
3
Ingredients
1 bottle Samuel Adams Boston Lager
2 Cups white flour
1 Cup whole wheat flour
1 Tablespoon baking powder
Directions
Combine ingredients in a large bowl and mix well. Evenly spread onto a greased 9 x 13&quot; baking pan. Sprinkle with your favorite sauce, toppings, and grated mozzarella cheese. Bake at 425?F for 25 to 30 minutes or until pizza is golden brown. Let stand about 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
