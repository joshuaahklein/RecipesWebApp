Meatloaf
Rating:
4
Ingredients
1½ lbs. ground beef
1cup breadcrumbs
1 medium onion chopped
1 green pepper, chopped
¼ cup celery chopped
1-½ teaspoon salt
¼ teaspoon pepper
½ can tomato sauce
Lightly mix above ingredients and form into a loaf. Place in shallow pan in 350º degree oven.
While starting to bake, combine the following sauce Ingredients:
½ can tomato sauce
2 tablespoons prepared mustard
2 tablespoons prepared mustard
2 tablespoons vinegar
2 tablespoons brown sugar or molasses
½ cup water
Directions
Pour over meatloaf.
Continue baking 1 to 1½ hours, basting occasionally.
