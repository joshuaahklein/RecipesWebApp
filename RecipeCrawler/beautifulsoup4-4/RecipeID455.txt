Pad Prik
Prep Time:
10 minutes
Servings:
1
Rating:
5
A delectable curry-based Thai dish with green beans and served with Jasmine rice.
Ingredients
6 ounces protein (beef, pork, chicken, tofu, or vegetables)
2 teaspoons oil
1 tablespoon red curry paste
1 teaspoon sugar
Kaffir lime leaves
1 cup fresh green beans (1 1/2-2 inches long)
1/2 cup water
Directions
Cook meat, tofu, or vegetables in oil until done.  Add red curry paste, sugar, lime leaves, green beans, and water; stir.  Reduce over heat until sauce is thickened.
Serve with Jasmine rice.
