Perfect Chocolate Hot Cocoa
Rating:
2
Ingredients
2 Tbsp. sugar
1 cup milk
23 tsp. Hershey's cocoa
1/4 tsp. vanilla extract
Dash of salt
Directions
Mix sugar, cocoa, and salt in large mug.
Heat milk in microwave on HIGH (100%) for 1 1/2 minutes or until hot.
Gradually add milk to cocoa mixture and stir well. Stir in vanilla.
