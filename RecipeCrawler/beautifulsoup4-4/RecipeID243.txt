Bombay Chicken and Rice
Prep Time:
20 minutes
Cook Time:
1 hour
Servings:
3-4
Rating:
5
Ingredients
1 cup uncooked long-grain white rice
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 (3 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
4 teaspoons curry powder, divided
6 ounces diced dried mixed fruit
1 1/2 teaspoons sugar
2 tablespoons butter, melted
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1 teaspoon salt
2 cups water
Directions
1.  Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
2.  In a 9 x 13 inch baking pan, mix rice, fruit onion, sugar  and salt.  Pour in water.  Arrange chicken parts over the rice mixture.  In a small bowl, mix butter, curry powder and paprika.  Brush butter over chicken pieces.  Cover pan tightly with aluminum foil.
3,  Bake 1 hour in the preheated oven, until chicken juices run clear and rice is tender.
