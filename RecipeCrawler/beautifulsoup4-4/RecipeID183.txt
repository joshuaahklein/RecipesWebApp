Smothered Steaks & Bell Peppers
Servings:
4 steaks
Rating:
2
Ingredients
4 beef cube steaks, about 5 oz each
1/2 teaspoons seasoned salt
1/2 teaspoon coarse ground black pepper
2 Tablespoons vegetable oil
1/2 cup green bell pepper, sliced
1/2 cup red bell pepper, sliced
1 small onion, sliced
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/3 cup Soy Sauce
2 Tablespoons chopped fresh parsely
2 cups uncooked Minute Rice, prepared as directed
Directions
Sprinkle steaks with salt and pepper,  Brown steaks in hot oil in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat 3 minutes on each side, remove and keep warm.  Saute' onions and bell peppers.  Blend together flour, soy sauce and 1 cup water, pour over onions and bell peppers.  Cook and stir until sauce boils and thickens.  Spoon over steaks, sprinkle with parsley.  Serve with rice.
