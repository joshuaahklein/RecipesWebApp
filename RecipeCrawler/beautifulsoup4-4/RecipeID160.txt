Better Than Sex Cake
Rating:
3
Ingredients
1 yellow cake mix with pudding
1 large can pineapple, crushed
1 cup sugar
1 pkg. Instant coconut pudding
Coconut
Nuts
Cool Whip
Directions
Mix and Bake cake per instructions on the box. While cake is baking, combine pineapple (do not drain), with 1 cup sugar; boil 5 minutes. Pour over hot cake; Cool. Mix coconut pudding as directed. Spread over cake and add Cool Whip. Sprinkle with nuts and coconut.
