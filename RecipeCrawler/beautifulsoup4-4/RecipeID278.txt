Sausage Cheese Puffs
Prep Time:
15 minutes
Cook Time:
25-30 minutes
Servings:
varied
Rating:
2
Ingredients
1 pound Original Sausage
2 cups biscut mix
2 1/2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup water
1 teaspoon baking powder
Directions
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Combine ingredients in large bowl until blended.  Shape into 1 inch balls.  Place on baking sheets.  Bake about 25 minutes or until golden brown.  Serve Hot.  Reffigerate leftovers.
