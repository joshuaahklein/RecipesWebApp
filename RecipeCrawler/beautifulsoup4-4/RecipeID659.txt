Angel Hair Capellini with Broccoli and Vegetables
Prep Time:
25 minutes
Servings:
4
Rating:
2
Ingredients
1 box angel hair pasta
4 Tbsp. extra virgin olive oil, divided
2 carrots, cut julienne style
1 zucchini, cut julienne style
1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
salt and pepper to taste
2 cups broccoli florets
1/4 cup Pecorino Romano cheese, shredded
Directions
Bring large pot of water to boil.  In a medium skillet, heal half of the olive oil and saute carrots for 4 minutes.  Then, add zucchini and cherry tomatoes and cook for 1 additional minute.  Season with salt and pepper.  Cook broccoli with pasta according to package directions.  Drain and toss with the vegetables.  Top with remaining olive oil and cheese before serving.
