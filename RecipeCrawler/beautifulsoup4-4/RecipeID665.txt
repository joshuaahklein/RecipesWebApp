Lettuce Wraps
Rating:
1
Ingredients
1 lb. ground turkey or chicken
Leaf lettuce
Teriyaki sauce
Flaked coconut
Chopped peanuts
Salt and pepper to taste
Directions
Cook turkey or chicken until well done.  Season with Teriyaki sauce, then toss flaked coconut and chopped peanuts.  Serve in lettuce cups.
