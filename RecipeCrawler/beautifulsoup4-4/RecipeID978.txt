Lemony Eggplant
Rating:
4
Ingredients
2 cups peeled eggplant, diced into 1/4 in pieces
2 cups lemon juice
2 cups sugar
Directions
Combine the lemon juice and sugar in a sauce pan.
Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to low.
Add eggplant, cook until translucent and soft to the touch.
