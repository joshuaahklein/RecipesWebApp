Bacon Wrapped Shrimp
Rating:
2
Ingredients
20 slices of bacon
2 lbs. shrimp
10 oz. teriyaki sauce
Directions
Cut bacon slices in half. Wrap bacon around each shrimp. Secure with toothpicks. Place in a 9 x 13-inch baking dish. Pour teriyaki sauce over shrimp. Cover and refrigerate 1 hour. Bake at 400, uncovered, 20 to 25 minutes or until bacon is crisp. Makes about 40 appetizers.
