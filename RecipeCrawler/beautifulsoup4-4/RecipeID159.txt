Banana Split Dessert
Rating:
2
Ingredients
9 x 13 graham cracker crust
Graham crackers, crushed
2 sticks oleo
3 eggs
1 box powdered sugar
Directions
Mix together for about 15 minutes.
Pour onto crust:
Drain well 2 large boxes of frozen strawberries. Cut 4-5 bananas and place on top of the strawberries. Add 2 (15-oz) cans of drained crushed pineapple. Cover with Cool Whip. Sprinkle with chopped nuts, and drizzle with chocolate syrup.
