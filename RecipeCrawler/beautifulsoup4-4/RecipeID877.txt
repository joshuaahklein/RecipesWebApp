Eurasian Braised Chicken
Servings:
4-6
Rating:
2
Ingredients
6 chicken thighs, skinned
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 large cloves garlic, minced
1/4 pound roma tomatoes, chunked
2/3 cup Kikkoman Sweet & Sour Sauce
2 tablespoons Kikkoman Soy Sauce
1 onion, chunked
1 package (9 oz.) frozen Italian green beans
Hot cooked rice
Directions
Coat chicken evenly with flour. Brown chicken in hot oil in large skillet over medium-high heat; remove.
Reduce heat; add garlic and saut√© until golden brown. Remove pan from heat; stir in tomatoes, sweet & sour sauce and soy sauce. Return chicken to pan; cover and simmer 20 minutes.
Stir in onion and green beans. Simmer, covered, 25 minutes longer, or until chicken is tender, stirring once halfway through cooking time.
Serve over rice.
