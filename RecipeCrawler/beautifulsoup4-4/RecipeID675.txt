Coca Cola Cake
Rating:
2
Ingredients
2 cups flour
2 cups sugar
1½ cup chopped marshmallows
½ shortening
½ cup margarine
3 tablespoons Coca
1 cup Coca-Cola or Pepsi
½ cup buttermilk
1 teaspoon soda
2 eggs beaten
Directions
Sift together flour and sugar, stir in marshmallows, set aside.
In sauce pan put shortening, butter Coca-Cola and coca.
Bring to a boil just until just melted (don’t beat), remove from heat and pour over flour mixture.
Stir in butter, milk, soda and eggs; pour into greased tube pan.
Bake at 350º for 45 minutes. Remove from oven and let cool in pan.
