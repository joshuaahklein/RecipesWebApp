Artichoke Dip
Servings:
2 1/2 cups dip
Rating:
1
Ingredients
1 can (13 3/4 ounces) hearts of artichokes, drained and chopped
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 garlic clove, pressed
Directions
In medium bowl combine artichokes, cheeses, mayonnaise and garlic. Spoon into a 9-inch pie plate or 3-cup capacity shallow baking dish spreading mixture evenly. Bake in a 350-degree oven for 15 to 18 minutes, until mixture is bubbly and lightly brown. Serve immediately with French bread slices or crackers.
