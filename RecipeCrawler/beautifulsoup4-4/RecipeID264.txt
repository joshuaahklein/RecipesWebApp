Savory Corn and Broccoli
Prep Time:
10 minutes
Cook Time:
35 minutes
Servings:
6
Rating:
4
Ingredients
1 can (14 3/4 oz.) cream-style sweet corn, no salt added
2 Tbsp. all-purpose flour
1 can (11 oz.) whole kernel corn, no salt added, drained
1 1/2 cups small broccoli florets
1/4 cup sliced green onions
2 egg whites or 1 egg, slightly beaten
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/4 cup (about 6 wafers) crushed low-salt shredded wheat wafers
Directions
Stir together cream corn and flour in a medium bowl until combined.  Stir in whole kernel corn, broccoli, onions, egg whites or egg, and pepper.
Spray a 9-inch pie plate with nonstick spray coating.  Spoon mixture into pie plate.  Sprinkle with wafers.
Bake at 350-degrees F 30 to 30 minutes or until set.
