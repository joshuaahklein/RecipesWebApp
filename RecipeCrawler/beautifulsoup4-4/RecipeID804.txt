Open Face Mushroom-Onion Burgers
Servings:
4
Rating:
2
Ingredients
1-1/4 pounds ground beef
1/4 cup Kikkoman Teriyaki Baste & Glaze
1/2 teaspoon pepper
6 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 medium onion, sliced
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 tablespoons Kikkoman Teriyaki Baste & Glaze
4 slices (1 oz. each) Monterey jack cheese
4 slices Italian loaf bread, grilled or toasted
Directions
Thoroughly combine ground beef, 1/4 cup teriyaki baste & glaze and pepper. Shape mixture into 4 oval patties.
Saut√© mushrooms and onion in hot oil in large skillet over medium heat 7 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in 2 Tbsp. teriyaki baste & glaze; keep warm.
Cook patties on grill 4 to 5 inches from hot coals 6 minutes. Turn over and top each patty with 1 cheese slice; cook 5 minutes longer, or until thoroughly cooked. (Or, broil patties 5 minutes on each side, or until thoroughly cooked, topping with cheese during last 30 seconds of cooking time.)
To serve, divide mushroom and onion mixture equally on bread slices; top with patties.
