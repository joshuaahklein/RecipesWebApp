Twice Baked Potatoes
Prep Time:
20-30 minutes
Servings:
8
Rating:
0
Ingredients
1 c. Sour Cream
1/2 c. Cheddar Cheese
1/2 c. Bacon bits
2 tsp Garlic Powder
2 tsp Black Pepper
1/2 c. (heaping) Chopped Onions
2 tbsp. Parsley
1 1/2 c. Instant Mashed Potatoes
1/2 C. Butter
Directions
Prepare potatoes as directed. add 1/2 c. butter. Let Cool slightly. Mix all ingredients together and place in SPRAYED pan. Bake 20-30 minutes at 350 Degrees.
