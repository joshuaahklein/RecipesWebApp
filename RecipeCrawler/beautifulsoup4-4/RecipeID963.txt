Italian Hoagies
Servings:
8
Rating:
3
Ingredients
32oz. Italian pork roast
8 hoagie rolls, split
1 cup marinara sauce
1/2 cup mild banana pepper rings, divided
1 cup shdredded Italian blend cheese
Directions
Prepare pork roast as package directs.  Shred cooked pork using two forms.  Divide and place hot pork on rolls.  Top with sauce.  Add pepper rings; sprinkle with cheese.
