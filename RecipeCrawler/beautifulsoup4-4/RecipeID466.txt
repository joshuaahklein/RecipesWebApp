Shrimp Potstickers
Prep Time:
15 minutes
Cook Time:
15 minutes
Servings:
24 - 30
Rating:
0
Ingredients
1/2 lb. uncooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
1/4 cup scallions, thinly sliced
1 large egg, lightly beaten
1/2 tsp course salt
1/4 tsp white pepper
24 - 30 round wonton wrappers
Directions
In a food processor pulse shrimp until ground.  Transfer to mixing bowl.  Add scallions, egg, salt, and pepper; mix well.
Place 1 tsp. of mixture onto wonton wrapper.  Brush edge with water, fold and seal.  In a large skillet or pot simmer water.  Place potstickers in simmering water approximately 5 minutes or until fully cooked.
Serve with choice of dipping sauce.
