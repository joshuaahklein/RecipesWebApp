Pomegranate Champagne Cocktail
Prep Time:
5 minutes
Servings:
1 serving
Rating:
1
Ingredients
1  ounce chilled pomegranate juice or chilled cranberry juice (2 tablespoons)
3  ounces chilled champagne (6 tablespoons)
Directions
1. Pour the pomegranate juice into a champagne flute. Slowly add champagne. Serve immediately.
