Broiled Turkey Slices with Quick Aioli
Servings:
4-6
Rating:
5
Ingredients
3 tablespoons Kikkoman Soy Sauce
3 tablespoons mayonnaise
1-1/2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1-1/2 teaspoons lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano, crumbled
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1 clove garlic, pressed
1 pound turkey breast slices
Directions
Slowly pour soy sauce into mayonnaise, stirring to blend. Combine with oil, lemon juice, oregano, pepper, garlic and 1 Tbsp. water.
Brush both sides of turkey thoroughly with portion of sauce; let stand 5 minutes.
Broil 6 to 8 inches from heat source 2 minutes.
Turn slices over; brush thoroughly with remaining sauce. Broil about 1 minute longer, or until no longer pink in center.
