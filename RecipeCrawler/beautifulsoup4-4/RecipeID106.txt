Lasagna
Servings:
8
Rating:
1
Ingredients
1 lb. Italian sausage
1 jar (16 oz.) chunky style pasta sauce
1 box (8 oz.) oven-ready lasagna noodles (12 noodles)
1 container (15 oz.) ricotta cheese
3 cups (12 oz.) shredded Mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Directions
Crumble and cook sausage in large skillet over medium heat until browned.  Remove from heat and stir in pasta sauce.  Preheat oven to 375-degrees F.  Spread 3/4 cup sauce into bottom of 9x13-inch baking dish.  Top with 3 noodles.  Cover noodles with 1/3 of ricotta cheese, 1 cup Mozzarella cheese, and 3/4 cup sauce.  Repeat layers two more times.  Add last 3 noodles.  Top with remaining sauce and Parmesan cheese.  Cover with foil and bake 35-40 minutes or until noodles are tender.  Refrigerate leftovers.
