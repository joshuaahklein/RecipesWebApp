Velveeta Cheesy Chicken Rotini
Prep Time:
10 minutes
Cook Time:
30 minutes
Servings:
6
Rating:
5
Ingredients
1 1/2 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into 1-inch pieces
2 cups water
3 cups rotini pasta, uncooked
1 package (16 oz.) frozen broccoli, cauliflower and carrot blend
1 can (10 oz.) Rotel diced tomatoes and green chilies, drained
1/2 lb. Velveeta cheese, cut into 1/2-inch cubes
Directions
Cook and stir chicken in large skillet sprayed with cooking spray on medium high heat 5 minutes or until no longer pink.
Stir in water.  Bring to a boil.  Add pasta, stir.  Cover, simmer on medium low 10 minutes or until pasta is tender.
Add remaining ingredients, stir.  Cook, covered, 5 to 7 minutes or until Velveeta is completely melted and vegetables are crisp-tender, stirring frequently.
