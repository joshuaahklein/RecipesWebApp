Lea & Perrins Burgers
Prep Time:
10 minutes
Cook Time:
10 minutes
Servings:
4
Rating:
1
Ingredients
1 pound ground beef
1/4 cup Lea & Perrins Worcestershire sauce
1/4 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. ground black pepper
Directions
In medium bowl, combine all ingredients.
Shape into 4 patties.
Grill or broil to desired doneness.
