Blackened Salmon
Prep Time:
5 minutes
Cook Time:
10 minutes
Servings:
4
Rating:
4
Ingredients
1 1/2 teaspoons Lea & Perrins Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1/4 teaspoon dried thyme leaves
2 teaspoons olive oil
4 salmon or cod fillets, about 8 oz each
Directions
In a small bowl, mix together Worcestershire Sauce, paprika, pepper and thyme. Drizzle salmon with oil and evenly coat fillets with seasoning mixture.
In a large skillet, sprayed with nonstick cooking spray then placed over medium heat, cook salmon for 3 to 5 minutes on each side, or until cooked to desired doneness.
