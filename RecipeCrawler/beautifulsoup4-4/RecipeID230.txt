Chicken & Noodle Casserole
Prep Time:
10 minutes
Cook Time:
25 minutes
Servings:
4 servings
Rating:
0
Ingredients
1 can (10 3/4 oz.) cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup milk
1 cup frozen peas
1 can (9.75 oz.) white premium chunk chicken breast in water, drained
2 cups medium egg noodles, cooked and drained
2 Tsp. dry bread crumbs
1 Tsp. butter, melted
Directions
Stir soup, milk, peas, chicken, and noodles in 1 1/2-quart casserole.
Bake at 400-degrees F for 20 minutes.  Stir.
Mix bread crumbs and butter and sprinkle on top.  Bake for 5 minutes more.
