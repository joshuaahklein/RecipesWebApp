Undone Stuffed Green Peppers
Cook Time:
25 minutes
Rating:
3
Ingredients
1 lb ground beef
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 green peppers, coarsely chopped
2 cups cooked long-grain white rice
1 jar (26 oz) spaghetti sauce
1 1/2 cups shredded cheese
Directions
Heat oven to 350.  Brown meat with peppers and garlic in large skillet; drain.  Stir in rice, spaghetti sauce and 3/4 cup cheese.  Spoon into 2-qt casserole; top with remaining cheese.  Bake 25 minutes, take out and enjoy!
