BBQ Pulled Pork
Prep Time:
10 minutes
Cook Time:
7-9 hours
Servings:
6-8
Rating:
0
Ingredients
2 1/2 - 5 pounds pork loin, trimmed of fat
2-4 cups barbecue sauce
2-3 onions, chopped
6-8 hamburger buns or hard rolls
Directions
Place pork loin in slow cooker.  Top with onion and barbecue sauce.
Cover and cook on low for 7-9 hours or on high for 4-5 hours, or until meat is tender.
Remove pork from slow cooker and shred with a fork.  Add pork back into slow cooker and coat with sauce.
Serve on hamburger buns or hard rolls.
