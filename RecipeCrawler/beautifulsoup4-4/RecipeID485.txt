Sugar Cookies
Rating:
2
Ingredients
6 oz Butter (room temperature)
5 ¼ oz Granulated Sugar
4 oz Brown Sugar
2 Tablespoon Corn Syrup
2 Tablespoon Samuel Adams Winter Lager
¼ teaspoon Nutmeg
1 ½ teaspoon Baking Powder
½ teaspoon Baking Soda
½ teaspoon Salt
1 Egg
10 ½ oz All-Purpose Flour
Directions
Mix granulated sugar, brown sugar and 2 Tablespoons of Samuel Adams Winter Lager in mixer (medium speed)
Add butter and cream until light and fluffy (medium/high speed)
Add corn syrup and continue to mix
Slow mixer down and add egg and then continue to mix on medium speed
Combine remaining ingredients in a separate bowl
Add dry ingredients to butter mixture on low speed until fully incorporated
Remove cookie dough from mixing bowl and wrap in plastic wrap and chill for 1 hour
BAKE:
Mix granulated sugar, brown sugar and 2 Tablespoons of Samuel Adams Winter Lager in mixer (medium speed)
Preheat oven to 350 degrees
Remove cookie dough from fridge and scoop 1 oz balls onto ungreased baking sheets
Bake for 8-10 minutes or until golden brown
Cool on baking sheet for 2 minutes; remove to a wire rake to completely cool
