Spinach-Stuffed Mushrooms
Prep Time:
15 minutes
Cook Time:
35 minutes
Servings:
40
Rating:
4
Ingredients
1 package (6 oz.) stuffing mix for chicken
1 1/2 cups hot water
40 fresh mushrooms (about 2 lbs.)
2 Tbsp. butter
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 package (10 oz.) frozen chopped spinach, thawed, well drained
1 cup shredded Mozzarella cheese
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Directions
Heat oven to 400-degrees F.  Mix stuffing mix and water.  Remove mushroom stems; chop stems.  Melt butter in skillet.  Add stems and garlic, cook and stir until tender.  Add to stuffing with spinach and cheeses; mix well.
Spoon mushroom caps.  Place in shallow baking pan.
Bake 20 minutes or until tender.
