WONDERFUL HAM AND POTATO CASSEROLE
Prep Time:
15-20 minutes
Cook Time:
1 hour
Servings:
6-8
Rating:
4
A simple, all-in-one meal for any occasion.
Ingredients
1  Pkg Shredded Hash Brown  (32 oz)
1 – 1/1/2 cups Ham, cubed
¾ stick of butter
2 Cups Sharp Cheddar Cheese, Shredded
1 Cup Sour Cream
1 Can Cream of Chicken Soup
2 Tablespoons Onion, minced
1½  teaspoons Salt
Pepper to taste
Directions
Mix all ingredients, hash browns and ham last. Bake in a 9 x 13 pan at 350 degrees for  approx. 1 hour or until bubbling in center.  Let stand for 10 – 15 minutes or until it firm.
