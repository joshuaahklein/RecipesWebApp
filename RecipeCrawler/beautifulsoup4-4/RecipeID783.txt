Potstickers with Ponzu-Sesame Dipping Sauce
Servings:
1/2 cup dipping sauce
Rating:
0
Ingredients
1/2 cup Kikkoman Ponzu
1 teaspoon Oriental sesame oil
1/4 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
1 package frozen potstickers or gyoza
Directions
Blend ponzu, sesame oil and pepper sauce together with wire whisk; pour into dipping bowls.
Cook potstickers according to package directions; serve with dipping sauce.
Note: Sauce can also be served with other types of steamed dumplings.
