Fruit Slush Punch
Rating:
0
Ingredients
2 1/2 quarts water
18 oz. frozen concentrated lemonade, undiluted
92 oz. unsweetened pineapple juice
2 pkgs. lemonlime or cherry Kool-Aid
80 oz. chilled ginger ale
Directions
Combine 1st 4 ingredients until mix dissolves. Pour into zip lock bags and freeze until ready to use. Tear away bags and put blocks in punch bowl 1 hour before serving and break into chunks. Add gingerale and serve.
