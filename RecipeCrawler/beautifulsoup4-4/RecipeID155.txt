Apple Muffins
Rating:
5
Ingredients
2 cups flour
1/2 tsp. Salt
4 tsp. Baking powder
1/4 cup sugar
3/4 tsp. Cinnamon
1/4 tsp. Nutmeg
1 egg, beaten
1 cup milk
1/3 cup oil
3/4-1 cup peeled chopped apple
Directions
Combine dry ingredients. Combine egg, milk, and oil. Add to dry ingredients, stir just to moisten. Add apples and mix. Fill greased muffin cups 2/3 full. Do not use cupcake papers. Bake at 350 for 25 minutes. Serve with honey. Add a little more sugar if not served with honey.
