Mix 'N Match Party Cheese Ball
Prep Time:
15 minutes
Cook Time:
1 hour 15 minutes
Servings:
26, or 3 1/4 cups
Rating:
3
Ingredients
2 packages (8 oz. each) cream cheese, softened
1 package (8 oz.) shredded sharp cheddar cheese
2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
4 slices bacon, cooked, crumbled
1 cup chopped mixed nuts
crackers
Directions
Mix all ingredients except nuts and crackers; cover.  Refrigerate 1 hour.
Shape into ball; roll in nuts.  Serve with crackers.
