Acini de Pepe Soup
Servings:
4
Rating:
1
Ingredients
8-9 oz. Acini de Pepe pasta
4 Tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese
1 sprig marjoram
1 pinch nutmeg
4 cups prepared broth
Salt and pepper to taste
Directions
Pour the broth in a large pot on the stove.  Add sprig of marjoram and bring to a boil.
Cook the pasta &quot;al dente&quot; according to package directions.  Drain.  Pour into soup bowl and serve piping hot, accompanied by the grated Parmesan cheese.
