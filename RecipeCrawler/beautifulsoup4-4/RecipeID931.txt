Beef Ribs Marinated In Red Wine Herb Sauce
Servings:
4
Rating:
2
Ingredients
SIGNATURE SAUCE
1 cup dry red wine (such as burgundy)
1/4 cup Kikkoman Soy Sauce
2 cloves minced garlic
2 teaspoons dried tarragon
1 teaspoon dried rosemary
1 teaspoon dried thyme
APPLICATION RECIPE
4 pounds meaty beef ribs
Directions
Marinate beef ribs in Signature Sauce, 4 hours or overnight.
Drain ribs, reserving marinade.
Place the marinated ribs over medium-hot coals or the medium heat of a gas grill.
Grill, basting with reserved marinade, until brown on all sides, 15 to 20 minutes. Do not overcook. Beef ribs should still be pink in the middle.
ADDITIONAL USAGE IDEAS
London broil marinated and basted with sauce. Cook and thicken sauce with 2 tablespoons cornstarch and serve as a sauce for grilled steak.
