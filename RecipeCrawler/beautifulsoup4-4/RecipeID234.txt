Beef Pot Roast
Rating:
0
Ingredients
3 medium potatoes, thinly sliced
2 large carrots, thinly sliced
1 onion, sliced
1 (3 to 4 lb.) boneless beef rump roast
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. ground black pepper
1/2 cup water -or- beef broth
Directions
Put vegetables in bottom of crockpot.  Salt and pepper meat, then place in pot on top of vegetables.  Add liquid.  Cover and cook on low 10 to 12 hours (5 to 6 hours on high.)
